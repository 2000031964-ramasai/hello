Agriculture Tools

Agriculture is the backbone of India. India is the second largest producer of wheat and rice, and many more. Agriculture is the main revenue for our country. Now a days Agriculture rate is decreasing day by day due to weather imbalance and no sufficient rains. To overcome these issues many new technologies came in to the market. Agriculture is the primary source of livelihood for about 58% of India's population.

Aquaculture Tools

In the world, India is the 2nd largest country in producing of fishes  and about 68% of India's fish comes from the aquaculture sector. Basically, Aquaculture is the farming in the water. Majorly, atmosphere conditions, water conditions, feeding play crucial role in aquaculture. The production of different varieties will depend on the marketing conditions.
